Bořivoi Mareček

Bořivoi in Tian Shan (1957)

Bořivoi was born on March 21st, 1917.  Bořivoi's father was Rudolf Mareček and his mother was Albina (Bela) Krauskopfova (Marečkova).  His paternal grandparents are Pavel Planka and Rozina Marečkova; his maternal grandparents are Tomàš Krauskopf and Eva Bauerova. He had three brothers and two sisters, named Miroslav, Vladivoj, Kvetoslav, Venceslava and Drahomira.  He was the third oldest of the six children.  He died at the age of 84 on May 8th, 2001.
