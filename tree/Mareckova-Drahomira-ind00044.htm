Drahomira Marečkova

Drahomira and Bela (1960)

Drahomira was born on February 12th, 1919.  Drahomira's father was Rudolf Mareček and her mother was Albina (Bela) Krauskopfova (Marečkova).  Her paternal grandparents are Pavel Planka and Rozina Marečkova; her maternal grandparents are Tomàš Krauskopf and Eva Bauerova. She had four brothers and a sister, named Miroslav, Bořivoi, Vladivoj, Kvetoslav and Venceslava.  She was the third youngest of the six children.  She died at the age of 85 on August 8th, 2004.
