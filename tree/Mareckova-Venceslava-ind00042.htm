Venceslava Marečkova

Rudolf, Bela and children (1915)
Venceslava was born in 1915.  Venceslava's father was Rudolf Mareček and her mother was Albina (Bela) Krauskopfova (Marečkova).  Her paternal grandparents are Pavel Planka and Rozina Marečkova; her maternal grandparents are Tomàš Krauskopf and Eva Bauerova. She had four brothers and a sister, named Miroslav, Bořivoi, Vladivoj, Kvetoslav and Drahomira.  She was the second oldest of the six children.  She died at the age of 3 in 1918.
