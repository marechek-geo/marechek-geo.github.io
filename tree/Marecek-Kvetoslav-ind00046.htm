Kvetoslav Mareček

Bela and Kvetoslav (1950)

Kvetoslav was born on July 30th, 1923.  Kvetoslav's father was Rudolf Mareček and his mother was Albina (Bela) Krauskopfova (Marečkova).  His paternal grandparents are Pavel Planka and Rozina Marečkova; his maternal grandparents are Tomàš Krauskopf and Eva Bauerova. He had three brothers and two sisters, named Miroslav, Bořivoi, Vladivoj, Venceslava and Drahomira.  He was the youngest of the six children.  He died at the age of 80 on June 26th, 2004.
