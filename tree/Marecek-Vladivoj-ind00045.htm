Vladivoj Mareček

Rudolf and family (1946)

Vladivoj was born on November 20th, 1920.  Vladivoj's father was Rudolf Mareček and his mother was Albina (Bela) Krauskopfova (Marečkova).  His paternal grandparents are Pavel Planka and Rozina Marečkova; his maternal grandparents are Tomàš Krauskopf and Eva Bauerova. He had three brothers and two sisters, named Miroslav, Bořivoi, Kvetoslav, Venceslava and Drahomira.  He was the second youngest of the six children.  He died at the age of 91 on September 3rd, 2012.
